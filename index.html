About Us

At NPHere, we believe in lighting up spaces with eco-friendly elegance. Our journey began with a single spark — the love for creating candles that not only illuminate homes but also inspire moments of peace and connection. What started as a small passion project has now grown into a brand trusted by many.

Every product is handcrafted with care, using sustainable materials, ensuring that your choice is kind to both your home and the planet.
